void calculateSummaries ( )

Calculates the summaries of the data and saves them to the correct arrays.

This part of the Naive Bayes algorithm summarizes the dataset. The summaries are the means and standard deviations of the data separated by class. Two subarrays are used to hold the summaries, their sizes are NUMBER_OF_CLASSES * NUMBER_OF_FEATURES. Therefore the first line of the arrays contain the summaries for the first class and so on and so forth
